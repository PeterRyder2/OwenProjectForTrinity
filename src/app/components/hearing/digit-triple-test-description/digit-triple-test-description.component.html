Hearing test instructions

During the test, you hear combinations of numbers, with noise in the background. Please enter the three numbers that you hear using the number pad on the screen. You may use the ← button to delete a number.

The number of times the process is repeated will depend on your particular responses.

The test will get harder. If you are unsure, please take a guess.

Press "Start" to begin the hearing test.
